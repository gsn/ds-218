Roundy’s Foundation

Chartered in 2003, the Roundy’s Foundation was created to further our commitment to the communities we serve. The Foundation is dedicated to hunger relief, domestic abuse prevention, and development of talent within the grocery industry. It has given more than $5.4 million to food banks and women and family violence shelters.
